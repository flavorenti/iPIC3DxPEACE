21/***************************************************************************
22 VCtopology3D.h - a 3D Virtual cartesian topology
23 A virtual topology is a mechanism for naming the processes
24 in a communicator in a way that fits the communication
25 pattern better. Since our processes will communicate mainly
26 with the nearest neighbours after the fashion of a two-dimensional
27 grid, we create a virtual topology to reflect this fact
